Relationships

Local and organic are positive trends in food. They keep more food dollars in the local economy, and improve the environment to ensure we can feed ourselves and the world long term.

Food is more than that, though. Eating food is personal -- the most basic act of trust. Wanting a direct relationship with the people who produce our food is just as human as wanting to eat local and organic.

Knowing your farmer makes a statement about who you are. You're a person who knows the picture of a farmer and red barn on a package of bacon is pure fiction. With fromMyFarmer, you don't need to settle for anonymous agriculture for sake of efficiency and convenience.

We make it easy to get food from folks you know, like, and trust.
